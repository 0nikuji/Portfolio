About

The artist

Jonathan Duenas

Jonathan Duenas creates images and identities that command attention. Based in Austin, Texas, he works at the intersection of photography and graphic design, documenting events as they happen and shaping brands into something people remember. Two years in, his work already has a voice: loud, intentional, and eye catching.

Self-taught and driven by instinct, his work spans a range of real world projects: from branding shoots for an intern team to covering Goodphil 2026 and documenting student organizations across campus. Every frame and every layout carries the same commitment: make it impossible to ignore.
